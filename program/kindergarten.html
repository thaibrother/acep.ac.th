Kindergarten Level · KG 1–3

A confident bilingual start at ACEP — where children flourish through play, build essential literacy and numeracy, and grow in character.

A Confident Bilingual Start at ACEP Kindergarten

At ACEP Kindergarten, we provide a nurturing environment where your child will flourish — developing confidence, bilingual proficiency, and a genuine love for learning. Our comprehensive program builds essential foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, creativity, and self-discipline, ensuring every child is thoroughly prepared for a seamless transition into our Primary English Program.

Why Choose ACEP Kindergarten?

The early years represent a precious window of opportunity for development. Our kindergarten curriculum is thoughtfully designed to:

  • Foster Natural English Acquisition — Through immersive, engaging activities, children develop strong English language skills alongside their Thai language development, becoming truly bilingual learners.
  • Inspire Through Play-Based Learning — Our students explore, discover, and grow through hands-on activities, creative play, and meaningful real-world experiences that make learning exciting.
  • Build Strong Character — Guided by our Catholic values, we emphasise kindness, respect, integrity, and compassion, shaping well-rounded individuals prepared for life's journey.
  • Develop 21st-Century Skills — From problem-solving and critical thinking to communication, collaboration, and creativity, we nurture the complete child for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Educational Philosophy

ACEP Kindergarten distinguishes itself through the seamless integration of proven international educational approaches — ensuring balanced development across intellectual, emotional, social, and spiritual dimensions. We prepare children not just for school, but for life.

Integration of internationally recognised educational approaches at ACEP Kindergarten

Learning by Doing — Meaningful, Experiential Education

We embrace the principle that children learn best through direct experience. Our Learning by Doing approach means:

  • Children learn through play, experimentation, movement, music, art, and daily life activities.
  • Knowledge and language are absorbed naturally and joyfully, not through rote memorisation.
  • We build strong foundational skills without rushing under academic pressure.
  • Every activity has purpose and meaning, connecting learning to real-world contexts.

This approach allows children to develop genuine understanding and lasting skills while maintaining their natural curiosity and love of learning.

Individualised Care in a Warm, Safe Environment

Shaping Dreams with Little Hands — Kindergarten activity

Every child is unique. Our approach includes:

  • Small class sizes — teachers know each child deeply.
  • Warm, nurturing relationships between teachers and students that build emotional security.
  • Respectful recognition of each child's individual strengths, needs, and learning pace.
  • Safe, supportive atmosphere where children feel confident to express themselves, take risks, and explore.

When children feel emotionally secure and valued, they learn with joy and confidence.
