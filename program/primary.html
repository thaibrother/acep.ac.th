Programs · Primary

Primary Level · Year 1–6

A strong English foundation and 21st-century skills from day one — empowering Thai children to thrive in a global world with solid academics and the moral discipline nurtured through Catholic values.

Primary Level Curriculum

At ACEP, we believe that building a strong English foundation and 21st-century skills must start from day one. Our goal is to empower Thai children to thrive in a globalised world with solid academics and the moral discipline nurtured through Catholic values.

What Do Students Learn?

ACEP Primary curriculum — core subjects taught by native-speaking teachers alongside Thai foundation

Our Primary Program (Years 1–6) follows the Thai Ministry of Education standards, integrated with international best practices. The curriculum fosters both academic excellence and a strong Thai identity through these core areas:

🌍 English Language Arts

Native-speaking teachers · English as the primary medium of instruction

🔢 Mathematics

Taught in English · international methodology

🔬 Science

Hands-on inquiry · English instruction

💻 ICT

Digital literacy from early grades

🎨 Arts & Music

Creative expression across mediums

⚕️ Health Education

Physical and mental wellbeing

🇹🇭 Thai Language

Strong Thai foundation · cultural identity

📚 History & Thai Culture

Heritage and civic understanding

✝️ Religion & Catholic Values

Moral formation in Saint Gabriel tradition

English Language Arts, Mathematics, and Science are taught by full-time foreign teachers and native-speaking teachers, with English as the primary medium of instruction. This ensures students gain practical English skills for real-life communication.
